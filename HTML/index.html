This course serves as an introduction to the basic concepts of Computer Science, laying a solid groundwork for students to pursue advanced courses in the field. It offers a comprehensive overview of the discipline, covering topics that help students grasp the functionalities of computer systems, including computer architecture, operating systems, computer networks, software and software development, data organization and visualization, as well as the possibilities of computing technology.
